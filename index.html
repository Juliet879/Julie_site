About me

Juliet is a 21 year old from Nairobi,Kenya currently a student in Software Development in AkiraChix based Nairobi. Originally am from Kisii ,from a family of six ,one brother and two sisters and the first born daughter. Am passionate about tech and how tech has diverse and alot of opportunities to grow and model people career wise. I belive in growth and embracing learning as the joyful part of life which makes me believe I can do it as long as I can learn it,so theres nothing difficult to it.
